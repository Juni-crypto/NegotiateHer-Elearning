Will I get a certificate?

Yes! Upon completing the course, you'll receive a personalized certificate of completion that you can add to your CV or LinkedIn profile.

You'll get lifetime access to all course materials, including any future updates. Learn at your own pace, on your own schedule.

Do I need prior experience?

No prior experience is needed! This course is designed to guide you step by step—whether you're just starting out or looking to sharpen your negotiation skills.

The course is delivered through our user-friendly online platform, accessible on any device. Each module contains video lessons, downloadable resources.

The course is self-paced, but we recommend setting aside 1-2 hours per week for optimal learning. Most students complete the course within 3-4 weeks.
